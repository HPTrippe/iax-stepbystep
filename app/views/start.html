How Certification Works

Anybody that wants to join the Identity and Attributes Exchange (IAX) as an identity or attribute provider or attribute consumer must be certified. An independent auditor manages the certification process.

As part of certification, you will need one of the following:

  • a remote audit
  • a remote audit plus an on-site audit

This depends on the role you want to play on the IAX and your risk level. Based on the evidence you provide, an auditor will let you know what’s right for you.
